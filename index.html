SAFETY GUIDLINES

  • Arrive at the bus stop at least five minutes early to avoid rushing.
  • Stay at a safe distance from the curb while waiting for the bus.
  • Wait for the bus to come to a complete stop before approaching it.
  • Use the handrail while getting on and off the bus to prevent falls.
  • Find a seat quickly and sit facing forward with your feet on the floor.
  • Keep the aisle clear of bags and other belongings to prevent tripping hazards.
  • Do not stick your hands, arms, or head out of the bus windows.
  • Stay seated and avoid distracting the bus driver during the ride.
  • Speak quietly and respect others on the bus.
  • Wait for the driver's signal before crossing the street after getting off the bus.
  • Cross in front of the bus, never behind it, and make sure the driver can see you.
